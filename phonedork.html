Hello, and thank you for using PhoneDork. Below are some simple instructions to get you working smoothly. We hope that this extension is successful in streamlining your workload.

• Highlight a phone number, right click, and execute the search.
• Each search will create a new tab.
• "Search All" will search Facebook, Google, and Twilio.
• To enter your Twilio account information: Click Here, and save.
